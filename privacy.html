Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 May 2026

This privacy policy applies to all apps published by Torro Make Apps. It explains how your data is handled when you use our apps.

Data collection

Our apps do not collect, transmit, or share any personal data with Torro Make Apps or any third party. All information you enter — such as project names, counter values, notes, and settings — is stored locally on your device.

Third-party services

Our apps do not use any analytics, advertising, or third-party SDKs that collect data.

Data storage and backup

Your data is stored in a local database on your device. It is not accessible to Torro Make Apps.

Your device's operating system may automatically back up app data to your personal cloud account (Google Drive on Android, iCloud on iOS) as part of its standard backup system. This backup is controlled entirely by you and your device settings — Torro Make Apps has no access to it.

Data deletion

Uninstalling an app and clearing its data removes all locally stored data from your device. Any cloud backup created by your device's backup system is managed through your Google or Apple account settings.

Children

Our apps do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, contact: torromakeapps@gmail.com
